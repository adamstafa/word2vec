in the beginning the idea of intelligent machines seemed distant and unrealistic many researchers worked on simple algorithms that could learn from small amounts of data over time computers became faster and storage became cheaper which allowed larger datasets to be collected people started sharing text from books articles and websites to train new models these models learned patterns in language such as grammar word order and meaning some systems could answer questions summarize documents or generate short stories others were used for translation speech recognition and recommendation systems as the field progressed scientists focused on improving efficiency scalability and fairness open datasets encouraged collaboration between universities companies and independent developers today machine learning is applied in healthcare education transportation and entertainment future research may explore reasoning creativity and interaction between humans and machines
